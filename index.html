Course Description
The purpose of the American Red Cross Lifeguarding course is to provide entry-level participants with the knowledge and skills to
recognize, respond to, prevent aquatic emergencies, and provide care for breathing, cardiac emergencies, injuries and sudden illnesses
until emergency medical services (EMS) take over. Participants who successfully complete this course receive American Red Cross
certification for Lifeguarding, First Aid, and CPR/AED valid for two years.


Course Materials
You must purchase a textbook and a pocket mask from the American Red Cross store.
Additionally, there will be no food breaks each class, so bring food.
